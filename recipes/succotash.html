Succotash

Image by stockking on Freepik

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons butter
  • 1/2 yellow onion diced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 red bell pepper diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper diced
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 1/2 cup tomatoes diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 2 cucumbers cut into cubes
  • 4 ounces fresh green beans cut into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cups corn

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil and butter in a skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir onion with a pinch of salt in hot butter and oil until onion is softened and slightly golden (6-7 minutes). Add red bell pepper, jalapeno, and garlic; cook and stir until pepper are fragrent and softened (3-4 minutes).
  2. Stir tomatoes, cumin, black pepper, and cayenne pepper into onion mixture. Cook and stir unitl heated through (1-3 minutes). Add cucumbers, green beans, water, and a pinch of salt. Cook 5-6 minutes. Stir corn into mixture until heated through (3-5 minutes). Season with salt.
  3. Serve and enjoy!
