Thai Curry Noodles With Chicken

Image by freepik

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons ginger minced
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 red chili pepper sliced
  • 1 tablespoon yellow curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 8 ounces cooked chicken chopped
  • 8 ounces egg noodles cooked al dente
  • Approx. 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon cilantro chopped

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in large pan over medium-high heat. Add ginger and garlic and saute for about 30 minutes
  2. Add sliced chili peppers and saute for another minute, then add curry paste and curry powder, and stir for another minute
  3. Sitr in chicken stock, cover pot, and bring everything to a boil
  4. Once boiling, add coconut milk and soy sauce, and bring back to boil
  5. Add chicken and noodles, cook everything uncovered until noodles are desired softness
  6. Turn off heat and stir in lime juice and cilantro
  7. Serve and enjoy!
